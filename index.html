About LIT-INN

The abbreviation “LIT” means Life is Today. LIT-INN is a life-style brand targeting aspiring, environmentally-minded, entrepreneurial, healthy and socially-responsible individuals (we call "Lit-inners").

LIT-INN is more than hotel, it’s a place where people could meet and interact, could work, stay, rest and enjoy facilities and do everything through their smart phones. In other words, it is a hotel and a club, including cafe-co-working, gym/SPA, bar/lounge and restaurant... all performed utilising latest technology.

"Life is Today" for us means four key elements which make up the Lit-Inn DNA:

  • Under one roof: hotel / co-living, co-working/cafe, SPA/health club, multiple entertainment areas.
  • Green: green buildings, renewable energy and smart planting.
  • Smart: utilising technology to enhance customer comfort and flexibility.
  • Local: we work with local community, including entrepreneurs, food producers, artists and craftsmen.

PARTNERSHIP PROGRAMS

Loan Notes

Loan Notes are secured investment products for Self-Certified Investors. Currently we have 2 programs in place, offering 14% and 18% per annum , depending on the type of note.

LoanNotes
Hotel Rooms

Investors could buy rooms at our establishments on a long-term lease basis (usually 125 years). Returns range between 6-12% + 105-125% buy-out option. There are also options for investors to partner with us on a profit sharing basis.

Hotel Rooms
Crowd-Funding

We are working with number of equity and debt crowd-funding platforms, offering investors varied returns depending on the type of products they choose. Expected returns for equity-based crowd-funding could be in excess of 20%.

Crowd-Funding
Buy-to-Let

We are also developing small apartments, called "Lit-Inn Residences", that could be sold on a freehold or leasehold basis, including all the Lit-Inn hotel features. These are sold with management contract, where returns could range between 5 and 15%, depending on location.

Buy-to-Let
Crypto

We are currently setting up an option for investor to participate in our hotel projects by buying digital tokens that are linked to the project. This option is for investors familiar with the crypto nand blockchain space

Crypto
Joint-Ventures

For larger institutional and other investors we are also able to offer bespoke solutions, such as joint-ventures and partnerships, depending on the investor's appetite.

Joint-Ventures
